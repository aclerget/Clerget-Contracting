Your story starts with a solid foundation

Clerget Contracting Ltd. Co. hails from the Big Sky state of Montana. Formed in 2023 by two college roommates with over 20 years of combined experience in foundation-up residential and commercial construction. Austin Clerget grew up in the agricultural industry which gives him an extensive background in ag-specific development from fencing to barns and shops plus everything in between. Ethan Digby hails from Canada and weighs in with a host of specialty expertise including drywall, electrical, concrete foundations, and full spectrum framing. Both have experience in the roofing industry.
